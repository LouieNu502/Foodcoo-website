Client Portal

Your personalized food dashboard

Once you're a client, Erin sets up a private portal just for your family — with your meal plan, meal feedback, and Erin's weekly notes all in one place.

How Your Portal Works

Every client gets their own private page, built by Erin after your initial consultation. Here's what's inside:

📋
Your Weekly Meal Plan 7 meals planned by Erin every week, tailored to your family's tastes, schedule, and nutrition goals.
🛒
Grocery Shopping, Handled Erin takes grocery shopping completely off your plate — whether that's ordering delivery or shopping in person, it's done for you.
⭐
Meal Feedback Loop Rate each meal and leave notes for Erin. Every response shapes the following week's plan.
✉️
A Note from Erin Every Week A personal message explaining the week's strategy, prep tips, and what to expect — written just for your family.
🔑 Your portal is private. Erin sets it up after your first consultation and sends you a direct link. It's yours alone — no account or password needed.
